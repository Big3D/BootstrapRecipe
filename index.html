Lets make some Goulash

A simple yet delicious recipe

Lets begin


We begin our journey with the core ingredients:

  • 1lb Ground beef
    - (you can use whatever grade you like)
  • Corn
    - (you can use fresh or canned)
  • Mac n Cheese
    - (I use Kraft, but its all preference)
See that was simple enough

Now onto the hard part...
COOKING!!!


Now you can do this is any order that suits you, but here is how I do it.
  1. Get a pan that can fit all the ingredients and cook the ground beef in it. I add a little bit of garlic power and chile powder to my meat right before its done.

  2. Next we are gonna tackle the mac n cheese, you can follow the box instructions. Ill add a little shredded cheese and paprika as I mix in the powder.

  3. Lastly we tackle the hardest of them all, CORN! Get your most rugged of can openers and rip the lid off. Then pour the contents into a pan and increase the heat till the kernels have felt a substantial amount of heat.

  4. THE FINAL STEP!!! Mix all the ingredients into the large pan you used for the meat (after you drained the grease), continue mixing on low heat to make sure everything is well mixed and hot.


  5. Oh wait I almost forgot the most important step... ENJOY!
